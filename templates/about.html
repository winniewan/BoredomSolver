About us

What do we do here at PF?

Our goal at PF is to help kill your boredem by finding events in your area that are availible to you. Throughout our sight you will find various events you can participate in when your feeling bored.

How do I use it?

If your're wondering how it all works it's quite simple! You input your location and activities you like to do. Then we match you with an activity for you to participate in that should match your interest.

What if we can't find you an event you're comfortable with?

Sometimes complications may arise where we cannot find you an event or activity taking place near you that falls under your preferred activities. To solve this we offer something outside of your comfort zone in the hopes forn you to try something new.

How does the finding people aspect work?

When entering in your likes and dislikes, through our site you have the opportuity to search for people who also share your interest so that you could go to these evets together.
